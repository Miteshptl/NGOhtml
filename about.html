About Us

Discover our story, mission, and vision for a better tomorrow.

Our Mission

To create a lasting impact in the lives of underprivileged children and communities by providing education, women empowerment, and livelihood opportunities.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to quality education, healthcare, and the opportunity to achieve their full potential.

Our History

2008 - Foundation

HelpingHands was founded with a vision to make education accessible for every child in rural India.

2012 - First School Partnership

Partnered with 5 local schools to provide scholarships, school supplies, and hygiene programs.

2017 - Women's Skill Center Launch

Launched our first Women's Empowerment Center offering tailoring, digital literacy, and small business training.

2023 - 10,000+ Lives Touched

Celebrated a major milestone: reaching over 10,000 individuals through our health, education, and livelihood programs.
